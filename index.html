Simple Ordering Process

From consultation to delivery, we make ordering custom rods straightforward and transparent

01

Consultation & Specification

Contact us with your requirements. We'll discuss blank options, guide configurations, grip styles, and custom wrapping details. For wholesale orders, we'll review quantities and pricing tiers. For custom builds, we'll create a detailed spec sheet tailored to your fishing style or charter needs.

02

Quote & Order Confirmation

Receive a detailed quote including materials, labor, and delivery timeframe. Once approved, we'll send an order confirmation with payment terms and expected completion date. For larger wholesale accounts, we can set up recurring orders and invoicing schedules that fit your business cycle.

03

Build & Quality Control

Your rods are built by experienced craftsmen using quality components and precision techniques. Each rod undergoes alignment checks, epoxy curing, and final inspections before packaging. For custom orders, we'll send progress photos at key stages so you can see the build quality firsthand.

04

Delivery & Support

Rods are carefully packaged and shipped to your location. We provide tracking information and coordinate delivery timing. After delivery, we offer ongoing support for warranty issues, repairs, and future orders. Partner accounts receive priority scheduling for rush builds and seasonal demand spikes.
